How to play

Game Rules

🎲Roll the dice to move your token. First to reach the final square wins!
❓Land on a special square — answer a question. Your result depends on your answer.
🤖Robot opponent answers with 70% accuracy. Stay sharp!
🏁Reach the END square and answer correctly to win!

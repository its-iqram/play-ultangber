Getting Started

How to Play

1️⃣ Select a question set and choose your board size and token colour, then tap Start Game.
2️⃣ Roll the dice on your turn. Your token moves forward by the number shown — e.g. rolling a 4 moves you 4 squares ahead.
3️⃣ Land on a coloured square? A challenge card question will appear. Answer correctly and the square effect works in your favour. Answer wrongly and the effect works against you.
4️⃣ Robot's turn happens automatically after yours. The robot answers with 70% accuracy — it won't always get it right!
5️⃣ Reach the END square and answer the final question correctly to win. A wrong answer sends you back 5 squares!
🏆 First player to land on the END square and answer correctly wins the game!

Special Squares

Challenge Squares Explained

🪜 Ladder

The bottom of a ladder is on this square. A question will be asked.

✅ Correct You climb up to the top of the ladder — skip ahead several squares!
❌ Wrong You miss the ladder and stay where you are.
🐍 Snake

You've landed on the snake's head! Answer to escape.

✅ Correct You escape the snake and stay safely on your square.
❌ Wrong The snake swallows you! You slide all the way down to the snake's tail.
➕ Positive Integer (Bonus)

A bonus square showing a positive number like +3 or +5.

✅ Correct Move forward by the number shown on the square. Closer to the end!
❌ Wrong No bonus — you stay on your current square.
➖ Negative Integer (Penalty)

A penalty square showing a negative number like −3 or −4.

✅ Correct You dodge the penalty and stay on your square. Phew!
❌ Wrong Move backward by the number shown. You lose ground!
🧊 Freeze

You're frozen! No question is asked when you first land here.

⚠️ On landing Your next turn is skipped automatically. You cannot roll.
✅ Correct (2nd turn) On the turn after the skip, a question appears. Answer correctly to unfreeze and roll normally.
❌ Wrong (2nd turn) You stay frozen. You'll be asked again on your next turn — until you get it right!
🏁 End Square (Final Square)

The last square on the board — but reaching it isn't enough to win!

🏆 Correct You win the game! Answer correctly and the victory is yours.
❌ Wrong Moved back 5 squares. You'll have to reach the END again and try once more.
